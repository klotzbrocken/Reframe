# Reframe 1.10.0

**You've Got Mail.** This release brings the iconic **AOL Desktop 4.0** to Reframe — in two flavours.

### The AOL Desktop 4.0 theme
A pixel-faithful recreation of the 1998 AOL client wrapped around today's web: the colourful two-row toolbar, the Keyword bar, the running-man throbber and the dial-in animation. Every page floats inside a real **MDI child window** you can move, resize, minimise and maximise — and several windows can be open at once. Service-only icons (Mail, People, Quotes…) are greyed out, as they'd be before you signed on.

### Windows & Mac OS 9
- **AOL Desktop 4.0** — the classic Windows look (Win98 window chrome).
- **AOL Desktop 4.0 (Mac OS 9)** — the Macintosh version, with classic Platinum title bars (close box on the left) and the menus in the real Mac menu bar.

### Channels that go somewhere
Click the **Channels** icon for the channel directory, or its **▼** for a quick drop-down — News, Sports, Travel, Shopping and more, each linking to a fitting modern site. **Right-click any channel to edit its link**; your choices are remembered.

### More polish
- With a window minimised, entering an address opens a **new window**.
- Navigation arrows light up **only when usable**; the address bar doubles as the Keyword box.
- Themes are now **sorted by year**, and every AOL screen has its own **About** page.
