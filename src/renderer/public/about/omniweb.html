OmniWeb 3.0

Version 3.0 · 1999
NeXTSTEP / Rhapsody · Mac OS 9 “Platinum” chrome

The Omni Group's browser for NeXTSTEP and early Mac OS — a hand-built interface of raised platinum buttons, cut pixel-precise from the original artwork.

About this recreation

This is a faithful reconstruction of the OmniWeb window chrome inside Reframe: the icon toolbar is assembled from the browser's own extracted button graphics, paired with the Mac OS 9 Platinum title bar (shared with Internet Explorer 4.01 for Macintosh). The page you are viewing, of course, is the live modern web.

Period details

  • Platinum title bar — close box at the far left; zoom and window-shade collapse boxes at the far right, just like Mac OS 9.
  • Raised icon toolbar — Back, Forward, Home, Open Location, New Window, Find, Links, Preferences, Page Info, Print and Reload, butted edge-to-edge.
  • “Page address:” field — bold monospace URL with a page-proxy drag handle and a recent-addresses chevron.
  • Hard-edged everywhere — every 3-D effect is a single-pixel highlight over a single-pixel shadow; no blur existed in this era.
