Communicator Pro 4.x · ~2000
Mac OS 9 “Platinum” chrome

Netscape's Communicator suite as it looked on the classic Mac — a raised platinum toolbar of bitmap buttons and the unmistakable black “N” throbber.

About this recreation

A pixel-faithful reconstruction of the Communicator 4.x Mac window chrome inside Reframe. The toolbar is built from the browser's own extracted 44×44 button bitmaps, paired with the Mac OS 9 Platinum title bar (shared with Internet Explorer 4.01 for Macintosh). The page itself is the live modern web.

Period details

  • Platinum title bar & frame — close box left; zoom and window-shade boxes right; a raised bevel around the whole window.
  • Bitmap toolbar — Back, Forward, Reload, Home, Search, Guide, Images, Print, Security and Stop, each a cropped original button.
  • The “N” throbber — the static Netscape N sits in its black box and animates while a page loads.
  • “Location :” bar — a sunken white URL field with the bookmark icon, exactly 16px tall.
