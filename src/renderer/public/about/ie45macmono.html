Internet Explorer 4.5
Macintosh Edition

Version 4.5 · 1-bit monochrome
Mac OS 8.5 · Appearance Manager “Platinum”

Microsoft's browser for the classic Mac — a flat Platinum button bar of little colour icons, and the unmistakable spinning “e”.

About this recreation

A high-fidelity reconstruction of the IE 4.5 Mac window chrome inside Reframe. The toolbar icons, throbber and status glyphs are the browser's own resources (decoded straight from the app's resource fork); the Mac OS 9 Platinum title bar is shared with Internet Explorer 4.01 for Macintosh. The page itself is the live modern web.

Period details

  • Platinum title bar & frame — close box left, zoom and collapse boxes right, a raised bevel around the window.
  • Flat button bar — Back, Forward, Stop, Refresh, Home, Favorites, History, Search, AutoFill, Larger, Smaller, Print, Mail, Preferences — each a 16×16 icon with a tiny label; the bevel appears only on hover.
  • The spinning “e” — the Explorer throbber (42 frames) sits idle and animates while a page loads.
  • “Address:” bar — a sunken white field in monospace with a history dropdown and a Go button.
